About

You can't ignore what your heart pumps for

Hello World! (cliche, I know)

My name is Randy... and I code.

I am a full-stack family-man who recently took a giant leap of faith when I packed my family up and moved from San Diego to Austin. Here, I would attend MakerSquare and pursue my dreams of learning to code, develop web apps, and ultimately find a career as a UI developer.

My previous professional experiences involved a commitment to working with the youth in a variety of educational settings. In my last position as a Site Supervisor for a literacy program, I made it my personal mission to promote ed-tech integration throughout our school district's classrooms. Within that movement, I discovered Codecademy and the fascinating world of web development; consequently, I made the decision to dedicate the rest of my career to being a developer.

The three-month journey through the MakerSquare Immersive Program (documented here in my blog) has been an amazing experience. I am most proud of learning new technologies at an accelerated pace and collaborating with some of the most brilliant and dedicated people I have ever met in my life. More importantly, during my time there, I was able to specificially identify what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.

What I love about UI development is the chance to combine my passions for art, design, and code! My future plans include mainly front-end work: increasing my knowledge of various CSS rameworks, researching/implementing UI essential concepts, continuing to build side-projects, and ultimately, the conquering of JavaScript.
