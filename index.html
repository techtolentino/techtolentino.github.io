About

you can't ignore what your heart pumps for

I am a full-stack family-man who recently took a giant leap of faith when I packed my family up and relocated with my wife and our two sons from San Diego to Austin. We moved to Austin to pursue my dreams of studying web-development at MakerSquare.

The three-month journey through the MakerSquare Immersive Program has been an indescribably amazing experience, and although it was very challenging at times, I loved every minute of it. I am most proud of learning new languages and technologies at a highly-accelerated pace in addition to collaborating with some of the most intellectual and dedicated people I have ever met in my life. More importantly, during my time at MKS, I was able to sample what it would be like to work in a problem-solving, collaborative, and tech-driven environment.

When I am not immersed in code, I love spending time with my wife and sons. We go on "family dates" and explore the city of Austin. My interests consist of writing, minimilastic art, hip-hop music, design, film-editing, and boxing (watching, not doing).

It's such an exciting time in my life! Ultimately, my career goal involves working alongside a creative team of developers and designers where I can contribute my skills and enthusiasm as a Front-End/UI developer! I look forward to sharing this momentum and positive energy with anyone who is willing.
