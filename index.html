About

You can't ignore what your heart pumps for

I am a full-stack family-man who recently took a giant leap of faith when I packed my family up and relocated with my wife and our two sons from San Diego to Austin. We moved to Austin to pursue my dreams of studying web-development at MakerSquare.

The three-month journey through the MakerSquare Immersive Program has been an indescribably amazing experience, and although it was very challenging at times, I loved every minute of it. I am most proud of learning new languages and technologies at a highly-accelerated pace in addition to collaborating with some of the most intellectual and dedicated people I have ever met in my life. More importantly, during my time at MKS, I was able to sample what it would be like to work in a problem-solving, collaborative, and tech-driven environment.

When I am not immersed in code, I love spending time with my wife and sons. We go on "family dates" and explore the city of Austin. My interests consist of writing, minimilastic art, hip-hop music, design, film-editing, and boxing (watching, not doing).

It's such an exciting time in my life! Ultimately, my career goal involves working alongside a creative team of developers and designers where I can contribute my skills and enthusiasm as a UI developer! I look forward to sharing this momentum and positive energy with anyone who is willing.

PORTFOLIO

SOFIA'S CATERING

The first landing page in the Stunt-End Development Project, Sofia's catering was a simple exercise in UI practices, ghost-button implementation, and responsive web design

OXCUTTERS

The second experiment from Stunt-End Development experimented with a centered, symmetrical design. This was an exercise in over-riding default Foundation styling

JOKE OF THE DAY

Designed the entire front-end of this app as part of the MakerSquare Hackathon 1 assignment by way of Foundation, JavaScript, and CSS-tricks to create lightbox effects

FRESH( );

Senior Project 1 - with the Fresh Function, users can create one of a kind code-related t-shirts. Used the Rails framework, Foundation for its grid, and JavaScript functions

CODE !SLEEP

Using the Skeleton boilerplate for the first time! CODE!Sleep collects your sleep info and calculates how much sleep to sacrifice in order to improve coding skills.

JUNIOR MENTOR

Senior Project 2 - designed and developed the entire UI for Junior Mentor, a platform which creates a supportive mentorship network within any organization.

WaxTracks Site

Part of the MakerSquare pre-work, our goal was to choose a small business and redesign their home page. I chose to redo WaxTracks, a small record shop in Madison, WI.

MKS Open House

This was a quick mock-up of a menu page that I did for the MakerSquare Open House event. This allowed users to catch my name, and later select an app to learn about.

FLATLIGN

Work in progress - a page that will allow users to select properties and create their own FLAT UI based landing page. Learning to use Highlight.js for code-grabs.
